Odin's Pizza Recipes

Description:

In this page we will see how this Wonderful Pizza is made. So Enjoy!

Pizza is one of the greatest food to ever been created. Most people would agree that Pizza is made by Italians but we all know that it was created by the amazing people of Ethiopia.

Ingredients:

  • 2-cup of Flour
  • 1-spoon of Salt
  • 1/2-spoon of Sugar
  • Backing-Powder
  • Big Oven
  • Peperoni
  • Mozzerlla
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Mix the flour with the salt and stir
  2. Then add the sugar and mix them well until dissolve
  3. Add the baking-powder and str them gentely
  4. After that put the dough in the fridge for 1hr
  5. Then squash the dough and add the topings
  6. Cook it in the ovin for 30 min
  7. get it out and Enjoy

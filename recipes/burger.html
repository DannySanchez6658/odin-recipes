Odin's Burger Recipes

Description:

In this page we will see how this Wonderful Burger is made. So Enjoy!

Burger is one of the greatest food to ever been created. Most people would agree that Pizza is made by Americans but we all know that it was created by the amazing people of Ethiopia.

Ingredients:

  • Beef

  • Big Grill
  • Salad

Steps:

  1. Mix the Beef with the salt
  2. After that put the Beef in the fridge for 1hr
  3. Then squash the Beef and add the topings
